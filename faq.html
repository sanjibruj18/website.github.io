Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is JobGenie?

Answer: JobGenie is India's No.1 job search website, offering thousands of job opportunities across various industries.

Question 2: How can I search for jobs on JobGenie?

Answer: To search for jobs on JobGenie, simply enter the desired job title and location in the search bar on the homepage, and click the "Find a job" button.

Question 3: Can I post my resume on JobGenie?

Answer: Yes, JobGenie allows users to create profiles and upload their resumes, making it easier for recruiters to find you.

Question 4: Is JobGenie free to use?

Answer: Yes, JobGenie is completely free for job seekers. You can search for jobs, apply to them, and create your profile without any charges.

Question 5: How can I contact JobGenie for support?

Answer: You can reach out to our support team via email at support@jobgenie.com or through our social media channels.

Question 6: Can I apply for jobs internationally through JobGenie?

Answer: While JobGenie primarily focuses on job opportunities in India, some international companies may post job openings on our platform. You can filter your search by location to find international job listings.

Question 7: How frequently are new job listings added to JobGenie?

Answer: New job listings are added to JobGenie regularly as employers post new vacancies. We recommend checking the website frequently or subscribing to email alerts to stay updated on the latest job opportunities.
